About Us

We are a boutique Travel Company with strong industry connections across the continents offering niche, handpicked range of services. Our vision is to provide our customers nothing but the best travel services. We cater to a growing demand in Special Interest Travel services, Leisure groups, Meetings & Incentives, Cruises, Festivals and Events.

Based in the United Arab Emirates with offices in Sri Lanka, Russia and representation in Europe and China, our travel veterans all share a single passion for creating and delivering memorable travel to our clients. We understand and appreciate diverse cultures in the furthest reaches of our unique planet and strive to take you there!

Tours


We are not just another Travel Company. Motivate Tours & Events is an experience provider and we firmly believe that a quality, memorable visitor experience is a shared outcome between our guests and us. In our carefully crafted products, we want to create meaning through direct experience. This experience includes the people met, places visited, activities participated and the memories created.

Our SIT initiatives are deep-rooted. We work with local communities in sharing their unique livelihood, the way of living, food, culture and heritage in a sustainable manner with our guests and share a reasonable amount of the profits made back to the same community with great respect for the local culture and social structures.

Incentives

Our unprecedented travel experiences are the ultimate in motivation and recognition for your employees or customers. By providing a total incentive travel solution including destination sourcing, hotel contract negotiation, meeting planning, event production, on-site program management, Motivate Tours & Events becomes a time and resource saving business partner for you.

Our services will allow you to target and focus your message towards your top performers or best customers while they are away from the clutter of their offices and daily lives. This is a chance to make them feel proud, special, celebrated, inspired and relaxed. Our tailor-made incentives range from a few nights stay to a spectacular weeklong escape at a magnificent destination anywhere in the world.

If you want to take your CSR on the road, we have ideal solutions. You will be surprised and humbled what a single dollar could do to community in a destination far from home. We work very closely with local communities to ensure that the authenticity of our client’s experience is actual. We channel their passion in what they know and do in their daily lives into sharing that knowledge and experience with our guests.

Incentive travel programs inspire and motivate people to their highest level of performance. As a niche travel incentive firm, Motivate Tours & Events creates programs like these every day for our clients. See what our incentive travel planning initiatives can do for you.



Meetings

If you are looking for experienced meeting planners to handle an entire event, our team works with you to create the best event possible.

Cultural Festivals


Festivals are a great way to contribute to the cultural sustainability of a nation. We take passion in sharing a rural dance form from the far north of Russia or an enthusiastic player of a traditional musical instrument from the mountains of Kazakhstan, magical drums and colorful dances of Sri Lanka or a traditional Oud player from Arabia with the world, an opportunity to share with the world the magical beauty of their culture and heritage.

In addition to our own annual festivals in UAE & Sri Lanka, we also offer cultural troupes, music & dance schools, soloists and social groups participation at various hand-picked festivals in Europe, Asia and the Middle East to celebrate their cultural diversity and heritage to make the world a better place for all.
